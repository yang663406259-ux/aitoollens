# About AIToolLens

AIToolLens is an independent technology review platform dedicated to helping professionals, creators, and developers find the best AI-powered tools for their workflows.

## Our Mission

The AI tools market is exploding with thousands of options. But most reviews are either paid promotions or too shallow to be useful. We started AIToolLens to cut through the noise and provide genuinely helpful guidance.

## What We Do

**We test** — Every tool gets a hands-on evaluation. We use it for real projects, measure its actual capabilities, and document what works (and what doesn't).

**We compare** — When two tools claim to do the same thing, we put them head-to-head with realistic use cases and transparent scoring.

**We explain** — AI is moving fast. We break down complex topics so you can make smart purchase decisions without needing a PhD.

## Our Standards

- **Hands-on testing over marketing claims.** We only recommend tools we've actually used.
- **No paid placements.** Our editorial rankings are never influenced by affiliate commissions or sponsorship.
- **Regular updates.** AI tools evolve rapidly. We revisit and update our reviews quarterly.
- **Transparency.** When we receive compensation from affiliate programs, we disclose it clearly.

## The Team

AIToolLens is run by a small team of writers and technologists with backgrounds in software engineering, product management, and digital marketing. We care deeply about accuracy and usefulness.
