AI meeting assistants have gone from novelty to necessity. In a world of endless Zoom calls, being able to get accurate transcripts, automated summaries, and action items without having to take notes changes how meetings work.

We tested Otter.ai, Fireflies, and Fathom on over 50 meetings to determine which is genuinely best.

## Quick Verdict

- **Fathom** wins for ease of use, generous free tier, and overall quality
- **Fireflies** wins for integrations and team collaboration features
- **Otter.ai** wins for long meetings and established enterprise features

## Pricing Comparison

| | Fathom | Fireflies | Otter.ai |
|---|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Free tier** | Unlimited meetings, 7 days retention | 3 meetings, 800 min storage | 300 min/month, 30 min/meeting |
| **Paid (individual)** | $24/month | $18/month | $16.99/month |
| **Team (per seat)** | $39/month | $39/month | $30/month |
| **Enterprise** | Available | Available | Available |

Fathom's free tier is genuinely unlimited — a significant advantage for individuals. Teams pricing is similar across tools.

## Transcription Accuracy

This is the most critical feature for any meeting assistant. We tested on:
- Standard business meetings (English)
- Meetings with multiple speakers
- Technical discussions with jargon
- Non-native English speakers

**Fathom:** Consistently accurate across all scenarios. Speaker identification was particularly strong — it correctly distinguished speakers even when they interrupted each other.

**Fireflies:** Very good accuracy, with strong handling of multi-speaker scenarios. Occasional issues distinguishing speakers who sounded similar.

**Otter.ai:** Accurate for single or two speakers, struggled more with large groups. The real-time transcription (live as you speak) is a unique advantage.

**Winner:** Fathom, narrowly.

## Summary Quality

Beyond transcription, these tools generate summaries, action items, and follow-up suggestions.

**Fathom:** Excellent summaries that feel genuinely useful rather than generic. The "AI highlights" feature identifies key moments and presents them with context. Action items are specific and actionable.

**Fireflies:** Good summaries, but occasionally miss nuance. The "AskFred" Q&A feature — asking questions about what was discussed — is a standout feature unique to Fireflies. You can ask "What did John say about the Q3 budget?" and get a specific answer.

**Otter.ai:** Decent summaries but feel more generic than competitors. Action items often require refinement. The "OtterPilot" feature for automatic slide capture is useful for presentations.

**Winner:** Fathom for summaries, Fireflies for Q&A capability.

## Integrations

Meeting assistants need to connect with your existing tools.

**Fathom:** Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Slack, Zapier. Good but not the most extensive.

**Fireflies:** 30+ integrations including all major CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce), project tools (Asana, Monday, Jira), HR tools, and note-taking apps. Easily the most comprehensive.

**Otter.ai:** Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Slack, Zapier. Plus native Chrome extension for web-based calls.

**Winner:** Fireflies, clearly.

## Team Collaboration

**Fathom:** Good basic sharing. Team can view recordings and add comments. Less mature team features than competitors.

**Fireflies:** Strong team workspace with shared meeting channels, comment threads on specific moments, playlist features for curating relevant meetings. Best for organizations that need to collaborate on meeting content.

**Otter.ai:** Mature team features with shared workspaces, meeting folders, and collaborative transcript editing. Good for large organizations with established workflows.

**Winner:** Fireflies for collaboration features.

## Unique Features

**Fathom:**
- Instant summaries available immediately after meeting ends
- Best mobile app for reviewing recordings on the go

**Fireflies:**
- AskFred Q&A across all your meetings
- Snippets and playlists for organizing meeting highlights
- Sentiment analysis

**Otter.ai:**
- Real-time transcription (live as meeting happens)
- AI chat to ask questions during recorded meetings
- OtterPilot automatic slide capture

## User Experience

**Fathom:** Clean, intuitive interface. Set up takes under 2 minutes. The least friction to start using.

**Fireflies:** Feature-rich but takes longer to learn. Web app is powerful but occasionally overwhelming with options.

**Otter.ai:** Mature, stable app. Slightly dated interface compared to competitors.

## Who Should Use Each?

**Fathom if you:**
- Want unlimited free meetings
- Prioritize ease of use
- Work solo or in small teams
- Value summary quality above all

**Fireflies if you:**
- Need integrations with many other tools
- Work in larger teams needing collaboration features
- Want the "AskFred" Q&A capability
- Handle sales calls with CRM integration needs

**Otter.ai if you:**
- Need real-time transcription during meetings
- Work in a large enterprise with established Otter workflows
- Attend many long meetings (2+ hours)
- Want the most mature, stable platform

## Final Recommendations

For most individual users and small teams, **Fathom** is the strongest choice. The unlimited free tier, excellent summaries, and smooth UX make it easy to recommend.

For teams with complex integration needs or sales organizations, **Fireflies** offers features that justify its paid plans.

For large enterprises with specific real-time transcription needs, **Otter.ai** remains a solid option despite feeling slightly behind on innovation.
